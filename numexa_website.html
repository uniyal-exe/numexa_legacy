Key Commands

A selection of the most-used commands for quick reference.

/calc <expression>

Evaluate a numeric or algebraic expression.

/integrate <expr>

Compute a symbolic integral.

/differentiate <expr>

Compute derivative.

/solve <eqn>

Solve equations or systems.

/matrix <...>

Perform matrix operations: inverse, determinant, eigenvalues.
